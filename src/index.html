profile

Hi! I'm Ahmed, a self-taught, front-end web developer currently working for Potato in London. I have over 3 years of experience creating mobile-first, responsive, cross-browser websites and interactive web apps. My tech stack currently includes HTML5, CSS3, SASS, ES6 Javascript, JQuery, Angular, Bootstrap, PHP & WordPress. I also have a lot of experience interacting with APIs using AJAX. I love learning new things and am also a creative guy that always challenges himself to add unique features to each project I work on.

In my past life I was a cleanroom process engineer at the Southampton Nanofabrication Center until I quit to pursue a coding career. Having learned many programming languages as part of my electronic engineering degree, such as C, VHDL and Verilog, I realised that I really enjoyed coding and I had a knack for it. I put my love for coding aside, however, and continued down the electronics route doing a PhD at the University of Southampton that led me to become a process engineer, a job that involved developing processes to create cool, tiny devices.

Being a creative person, I enjoyed the job at first, especially setting up the processes, but over time there was less developing and more running of processes with monotony replacing creativity and indifference replacing joy. So I decided to make a drastic change and quit. Well, it wasn't as spontaneous as that because I'm also a methodical guy. I spent over a year teaching myself to code in my spare time and as an engineer I focused on learning to write efficient, succinct code with an emphasis on speed and minimising redundancy since I absolutely hate redundancy it. Learning the quirks and tricks behind cross-browser compatibility was also an xhausting xperience. I began to freelance part-time as a web developer to enhance my skills and only after I was confindent of my ability to independently create full websites from scratch did I quit my job and make my leap to full-time freelancing. Eventually in 2017 I started working for digital agency Potato in London as a Front-end Developer.

If you're interested to know more about me click on the links below to see my journey from electronic engineer to web developer or, for legacy reasons, my past life as a cleanroom process engineer. You can also have a look at my CV or feel free to visit my GitHub profile and see the code I've written for some of my projects. If you'd like to get in touch with me please e-mail me at aoabuelgasim@gmail.com or use the contact form below.
